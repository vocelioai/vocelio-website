Frequently Asked Questions

💳 How does the "pay per successful call" model work?

You only pay $0.20 when a call achieves a positive outcome (appointment booked, sale made, lead qualified, etc.). Failed calls, hang-ups, and voicemails are completely free. This aligns our success with yours - we only make money when you do.

🆓 Is the free trial really free?

Yes! You get 100 completely free AI calls with full access to all features. No credit card required, no hidden fees, no time limits. Test everything risk-free and see the results for yourself before committing to anything.

📈 What counts as a "successful call"?

A successful call is one where the prospect takes a desired action: books an appointment, requests more information, agrees to a follow-up, makes a purchase, or any other positive outcome you define. You have full control over what constitutes success for your business.

🔄 Can I cancel or change plans anytime?

Absolutely! There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time. With pay-per-call pricing, you're never locked into expensive monthly commitments.

🌍 Do you support international calling?

Yes! We support calling to 200+ countries with local number provisioning in most regions. Our AI agents speak 13+ languages fluently, and we handle all international compliance requirements including TCPA, GDPR, and local regulations.

🛡️ Is my data secure and compliant?

Security is our top priority. We're SOC 2 Type II certified, TCPA/GDPR compliant, and use bank-level encryption. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, with comprehensive audit trails and role-based access controls.

⚡ How quickly can I get started?

Setup takes just 5 minutes! Create your account, customize your AI agent's script and voice, upload your contacts, and start making calls immediately. No technical knowledge required - our intuitive interface makes it easy for anyone to get started.

📞 What if I need to transfer calls to humans?

No problem! Our AI agents can seamlessly transfer calls to your human team members with full conversation context preserved. You can set custom transfer triggers and maintain a hybrid approach that leverages both AI efficiency and human expertise.
